About MediSight AI

MediSight AI is designed as a doctor-assistive project concept for multimodal triage support.

Project Purpose

This frontend demo shows how AI-assisted workflows can combine image findings, patient vitals, and symptom text into a structured clinical decision-support summary.

How It Works
  1. Upload and categorize a clinical image.
  2. Enter patient vitals and relevant physiology values.
  3. Describe symptoms in natural language.
  4. Review mock AI output for physician interpretation.
Ethical Note

This system is educational and frontend-only.

  • No live diagnosis is generated.
  • No patient data is stored securely in this demo.
  • Clinical decisions must remain physician-led.
